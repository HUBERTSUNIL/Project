ABOUT


This Website is based on a restaurant in Kerala named USTAD HOTEL.
In the landing Page you can see the restaurant's main page in which it gives a brief info about the restaurant.
If you click HOME button on the navigation bar it will take you to to the home page.
If you click EXPLORE it will take you to a page in which you will see different food images among which the top three is accessible and will take you to their respective pages.
The First Image takes you to page where you can order different SADYA and PAYASAM.
The Second Image takes you to the juice page which you can order the different types of juices available.
The Third Image takes you to the page where you can order different Biriyani that you prefer.
On the bottom of the pages you can see a footer in which you can follow our social media platforms and also a support box.
